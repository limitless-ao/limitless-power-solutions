Business model

Community Microgrids

Revenue

Unit 03

Hybrid AC/DC integrated renewable system replacing diesel generators.

Revenue

  • · Research grants

  • · Pilot deployment contracts
  • · Licensing (long term)

Grant readiness

Built to meet international standards.

  • ✓ Scalable architecture
  • ✓ Local supply-chain utilization
