## 2. Key operational components

| Function | Owner (today) | Scale path |
|---|---|---|
| **Engineering & design** | Founder + freelance engineers | Hire lead engineer once recurring revenue covers salary |
| **Installation** | Subcontracted certified installers | Build owned crew in core service zone (Osun + Lagos) |
| **Procurement** | Local distributors (Tier-1 panels, certified inverters) | Direct importer relationships at scale |
| **Maintenance & support** | Founder + on-call technicians | 24/7 dispatch + ticketing system |
| **Sales & business dev** | Founder | Hire BD lead post Series-A / equivalent |
| **Finance & admin** | Founder + accountant | Outsourced bookkeeping → in-house controller |
| **R&D** | Founder + university / accelerator partners | Dedicated R&D engineer once first grant lands |
| **Brand & comms** | This website + LinkedIn | Add quarterly impact reports + investor updates |

## 3. Revenue streams

### Unit 01 — Commercial Solar
- **System sales** — one-off capex, 1 KVA → 10 KVA+
- **Installation fees** — bundled or separately quoted
- **Annual maintenance contracts** — recurring, ~10–15% of system value/year
- **Energy-as-a-Service / lease-to-own** — medium-term option for cash-constrained customers
- **Performance audits** — paid one-off engagements

### Unit 02 — Community Microgrids (SustainGrid)
- **Mobile-money tariffs** — pay-as-you-go from end-users
- **Grant funding** — climate, development, energy-access programs
- **Government / NGO contracts** — public-sector procurement
- **Carbon credits** — once measurement & verification systems are in place

### Unit 03 — Research & Innovation
- **Innovation grants** — ZE-Gen-style accelerators, climate funds
- **Pilot deployment contracts** — paid demonstrations
- **Technology licensing** — long-horizon, post-validation

## 4. Strategic positioning

**Where we play:** Nigeria first, with a pan-African ambition. Prioritize Osun & southwest Nigeria for geographic density before national expansion.

**How we win:**
1. **Engineering credibility** — load-first design, honest sizing, transparent warranties.
2. **Local supply chains** — trained Nigerian technicians; spare-parts availability matters.
3. **Diversified revenue** — commercial cashflow funds operations; grants fund R&D; project finance funds microgrids.
4. **Measurable impact** — every system reports kWh, diesel displaced, CO₂ avoided.

**What we won't do:**
- Compete on price by cutting component quality.
- Take on diesel-only contracts.
- Over-promise system longevity beyond what we can warranty.

## 5. Growth phases

| Phase | Horizon | Goal | Funding |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Foundation** | 0–6 months | Land first 5 commercial customers, formalize processes, ship website + brand | Founder capital + first revenue |
| **Validate** | 6–18 months | 20+ commercial installs, first microgrid pilot, first innovation-grant submission | Angel / convertible note |
| **Scale** | 18–36 months | Multi-state commercial coverage, 3–5 microgrid sites, R&D prototype | Project finance + innovation grant |
| **Expand** | 36 months+ | Pan-African pilots, licensing conversations | DFI debt + carbon finance |

## 6. Governance roadmap

- **Today:** Sole proprietorship — appropriate for early stage, simple to operate.
- **Trigger to incorporate (Ltd):** First institutional investment, first government contract, or first grant >₦20m. Convert to a CAC-registered company limited by shares.
- **Then:** Add an advisory board (energy + finance + community-development representation), then a formal board once equity is taken on.
