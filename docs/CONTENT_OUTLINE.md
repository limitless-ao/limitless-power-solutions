# Content Outline — Limitless Power Solutions

Per-page brief: purpose, audience, key messages, photography needs.

---

## 1. Home (`index.html`)
**Purpose:** Frame the company in 30 seconds — what we do, for whom, with credibility cues.
**Audience:** Mixed (B2B buyers, investors, partners).
**Key sections:** Hero · trust strip · 4-service overview · approach principles · two innovation initiatives · process · CTA strip.
**Photo needs:** Founder/team at a tech-hub rooftop install (hero supplement); short sequence of installs.

## 2. About (`about.html`)
**Purpose:** Establish legitimacy — registered company, real founder, clear mission.
**Audience:** All — especially investors and grant reviewers.
**Key sections:** Mission · Vision · Story · Registration card (BN, TIN, address) · Principles · Founder profile.
**Photo needs:** Founder portrait. Optional: team / office shot.

## 3. Services (`services.html`)
**Purpose:** Translate capabilities into purchasable services with clear scope.
**Audience:** B2B buyers (tech hubs, SMEs, clinics, offices).
**Key sections:** 4 service deep-dives (Solar, Storage, EMS, Support) · capacity-tier table · CTA.
**Photo needs:** System schematic diagram, battery bank, EMS dashboard mock, technician on a service visit.

## 4. Microgrid (`microgrid.html`)
**Purpose:** Position SustainGrid program for development partners and grant reviewers.
**Audience:** NGOs, DFIs, state government, climate funders.
**Key sections:** Rationale · 9-step methodology · pilot scenario · expected outcomes · partner CTA.
**Photo needs:** Rural community context, pilot site (when live).

## 5. Research (`research.html`)
**Purpose:** Demonstrate R&D rigour for innovation grants (ZE-Gen-style).
**Audience:** Climate-innovation accelerators, technical reviewers.
**Key sections:** Executive summary · 9 objectives · 6-phase methodology · outcomes.
**Photo needs:** R&D bench / prototype (when live). Technical diagrams welcome.

## 6. Projects (`projects.html`)
**Purpose:** Show momentum and case-study quality even before a large portfolio exists.
**Audience:** Buyers + funders.
**Key sections:** 3 sample projects (one per track) · case-study format spec · pilot-site CTA.
**Photo needs:** Project photos as deployments go live.

## 7. Impact (`impact.html`)
**Purpose:** Make impact measurable and grant-credible.
**Audience:** Funders, partners, ESG-conscious customers.
**Key sections:** 3-pillar framework · SDG alignment · circularity plan.
**Photo needs:** None required (icon-led).

## 8. Investors (`investors.html`)
**Purpose:** Convert serious funders into a meeting.
**Audience:** Angels, family offices, DFIs, accelerators, government procurement.
**Key sections:** 3-unit business model · 5 funding tracks · grant-readiness checklist · data-room request.
**Photo needs:** None required.

## 9. Contact (`contact.html`)
**Purpose:** Clear, frictionless capture of inbound interest, segmented by intent.
**Audience:** All.
**Key sections:** Direct channels · contact form (with reason dropdown) · audience-specific guidance cards.
**Photo needs:** Optional office exterior.

---

## Voice & tone

- **Confident & corporate, but warm.** No buzzwords ("synergy", "best-in-class"). Direct claims.
- **Engineering-led.** Talk in kWh, kVA, payback periods, downtime hours — not "innovative solutions".
- **Honest about stage.** A young company. Pilot sites are pilots. Don't oversell.

## Things to avoid

- Stock photos of generic data centers or clip-art solar panels.
- Made-up customer logos / testimonials.
- Vague impact claims (no "X% improvement" without a real number).
- Diesel-positive language. Diesel is a fallback, never a feature.
